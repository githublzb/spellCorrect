奥巴马在中国宣布他从来没有使用过twitter（今日techcrunch头条）President Obama Admits That He&rsquo;s Never Used Twitter, But Thinks The Chinese Should Be Able To 


   by MG Siegler on November 15, 2009 美国总统奥巴马承认他从未使用过Twitter，但他认为中国应该开放使用。


   President Barack Obama has one of the most popular Twitter accounts with over 2.6 million followers. It should be no real surprise that most of the time it&rsquo;s not him tweeting from it, instead its various people within the White House communication team that use the account to send out information. And now that he is the President, certainly there are some security concerns with him using something like Twitter. But, did you know that he&rsquo;s actually never used Twitter at all? 美国总统奥巴马有一个260万人访问的，很时髦的Twitter账号。但是如果你知道大部分时间都不是他本人在使用的话，也不要感到惊讶，实际上白宫通讯组的各种人物都在使用这个账号发布信息。现在他已经是美国总统，那么继续使用诸如Twitter这样的个人工具，就需要有一些安全性因素的考虑。但是，但是你知道么他居然从来没有用过Twitter。


   That revelation was made tonight during a Q&amp;A session at a town hall event with Chinese youth that was held in Shanghai this evening (which was streamed live on the web). The President fielded a question about the restricted use of Twitter in China and he had this to say, &ldquo;I have never used Twitter but I&rsquo;m an advocate of technology and not restricting internet access.&rdquo;这个秘密是奥巴马在上海与中国年轻人在一个市政厅活动的Q&amp;A问答中被解密的。这个过程已经被发布到了网络上。总统被问到了一个关于在中国禁止使用Twitter账号的问题，在回答这个问题时，奥巴马说：&ldquo;我从来没有用过Twitter，但是我是一个新技术的倡导者，从来不限制互联网的活动。&rdquo;


   This is interesting considering the Internet, and social media in particular, was considered a large part of his ascension to the Presidency. Obviously, he had a killer team around him that was able to embrace the web without the then-Senator getting too much involved. Still, it&rsquo;s somewhat surprising that he never sent any of his own tweets during the primaries. And undoubtedly part of us wants to believe that when you see tweets like &ldquo;This is history,&rdquo; which was sent on November 7 &mdash; or &ldquo;Humbled&rdquo; after he won the Nobel Peace Prize in October, that&rsquo;s it could the President really sending it. Nope.这是互联网的一个热议话题，对于社会媒体更是如此，因为这是来自一位美国总统的言论。显然，奥巴马有一支强有力的队伍在支持他，这支队伍能够充分接触到互联网，不需要参议员们过多触网。但是令人惊讶的是在他总统竞选(primaries)的时候也没有使用过tweet。毫无疑问，在他10月获得诺贝尔和平奖后，我们相信在tweet中看到了诸如&ldquo;这是一个历史时刻&rdquo;(11月7日)，或者&ldquo;我很卑微&rdquo;这样的关键词的确是奥巴马本人所发。


   Of course, the more important story here is his stance on Chinese Internet censorship. It&rsquo;s ridiculous that people in China are restricted from accessing certain parts of the Internet. But we&rsquo;ve all known that for a long time, and we&rsquo;ve known the President&rsquo;s position on it.当然，这个故事最重要的还是他关于中国互联网新闻省察的观点。奇怪的是中国的大众被限制访问互联网的某些内容。但是我们都知道这已经是古已有之，我们也很清楚总统对于此事的立场。
