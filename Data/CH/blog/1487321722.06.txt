谷歌雅虎新闻大战-两种路线的PK《旧金山记事报》7月11日发文，雅虎推出Upshot【http://news.yahoo.com/upshot】。


   Last week, Yahoo unveiled the Upshot, a blog created by the Sunnyvale portal's growing staff of editors and reporters, who will use search and other user data to help determine what they'll cover and how. 


   


   雅虎的战略是内容战略，通过聚集对的人（内容编辑），在对的时间，发布对的内容，为广大群众服务，把用户留在自己的流量圈内。


   ，


   谷歌的战略是技术战略，通过聚集对的人（技术人员），对不同的用户，发布个性化的内容，为广大群众服务，把用户推给别人。


   


   文章观点：


   雅虎：Yahoo's new blog highlights its transformation into a 21st century media company, a destination that uses a combination of technology and humans to find or create the content people want. 


   


   谷歌：Meanwhile, Google continues to demonstrate an unflinching faith in the ability of increasingly sophisticated search technology to evaluate and point to the most relevant content from across the Internet.


   雅虎一家做大，谷歌协众做大，两种理念，两种路线，作者总结的很好。
